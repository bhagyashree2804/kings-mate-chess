About Kings Mate Chess Academy

We are one of India’s premier online chess academies, dedicated to nurturing young minds with confidence, discipline, and strategic thinking.

At Kings Mate Chess Academy, we combine structured curriculum, expert coaching, and interactive sessions to ensure every student receives personalized attention.

Our programs cater to all levels — from absolute beginners to advanced players — helping students build strong fundamentals, tactical sharpness, and tournament readiness.

Chess is more than a game to us — it’s a tool to build focus, patience, problem-solving, and lifelong confidence.
